Science Explainer

The research is based on specific scientific methods and biological markers. Here are brief explanations of the key terms:

What is a "knockdown" study?

A research method where scientists reduce a specific gene's expression to understand its function. By observing what happens when the gene is less active, they can determine its role in biological processes.

What are these markers?

  • BMAL1: A core "clock gene" that acts as a master regulator of the circadian rhythm in cells.
  • IL-1α & IL-6/TNFα: Cytokine proteins that are key indicators of an inflammatory response.
  • P21: A protein that acts as a stop signal for cell division; a well-known marker for cellular aging.
